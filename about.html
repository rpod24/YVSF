Zihua Wang

Chief Operations Officer

Zihua Wang is a junior at the Harker School with a longtime passion for service. In middle school, he began volunteering regularly for local organizations including churches and holiday gift drives where he learned the importance of volunteer work. Inspired to do more, Zihua decided to take more initiative planning and leading his own service projects in high school. Among his accomplishments are organizing a conservation project for a local park and leading a PPE-manufacturing group during the COVID-19 crisis. With YVSF, Zihua hopes to bring his experience with service at the local level into a wider community and fight a pressing issue plaguing thousands of Americans.
